about the client

brushing buddies is an innovative sing-along sound book designed to transform toothbrushing from a daily struggle into a joyful experience. created by a mom inspired by her daughter, brushing buddies uses music, storytelling, and positive reinforcement to make brushing both fun and educational.

the company's approach is research-backed, developed in collaboration with the university of pennsylvania, ensuring its methods are grounded in motivational and learning science.

the challenge

how might we...

redesign brushing buddies' website and logo to create a playful, trustworthy brand identity that empowers parents and engages young children?

research & insights

before beginning our redesign, we analyzed similar brands in the children's education, wellness, and product spaces. our goal was to understand how others communicate trust, playfulness, and parent engagement.

  • storytelling through visual identity: brands that succeed with parents use cohesive narratives across all touchpoints
  • care and emotion in all products and visuals: parents respond to warmth and authenticity in design
  • clean, simple and trustworthy content: clarity and professionalism build confidence in the product

moodboards & exploration

building on our research insights, we created moodboards to explore visual directions that capture brushing buddies' joyful, nurturing, and research-backed identity. these boards guided our choices in color, typography, imagery, and tone for the new brand system.

playful & innovative

conveyed a playful, positive vibe that honors youth, creativity, and inclusivity. bold and rounded typefaces with vibrant, welcoming colors. combined childlike energy with modern design trends using soft shapes and approachable sans-serif fonts.

calming & approachable

created something both playful yet calming to draw attention while emphasizing a smoother teeth-brushing experience. squiggly lines reminiscent of toothpaste with pops of pastel color‚Äìvibrant but calming.

bright & friendly

bright and playful identity appealing to kids and parents using organic/rounded shapes, cheerful illustrations, and vibrant palette of greens, blues, yellows, and pinks. bold sans serif headings paired with lighter text kept it clean and friendly.

imaginative & textured

evoked childhood through playful texture, color, and form. emphasized imagination and curiosity, relevant to encouraging children through storytelling. hand-drawn elements and organic shapes appealed to both children and adults.

color palette

our color palette is designed to appeal to millennial and gen z parents‚Äìmodern and approachable‚Äìwhile still engaging for young children. softer pastels resonate with the nurturing, calm world of ages 2‚Äì4, while brighter, more vibrant hues energize and inspire creativity for ages 4 and up.
